# Copy Headline Reference

Use these as reusable patterns for future sales pages and niche adaptations.

## Hook Structures

- Are you [desire/identity]... but [main frustration or uncertainty]?
- Ever feel like [problem]... and [secondary audience problem]?
- Are you [excited/dreaming/hungry]... but [blocked by confusion]?
- Still [wasting/struggling] with [painful task]?
- Have you ever felt like [deeper emotional tension]?
- What if [desired transformation] could be [simple/enjoyable/fast]?

## Example Hooks

- Are you hungry to understand your faith more deeply... but overwhelmed by where to start?
- Are you holding your breath every time your doctor brings up your liver... but still have no clue what you're actually supposed to eat?
- Are you excited about your Cricut... but secretly frustrated every time you turn it on?
- Are you over 60 and worried that eating "healthy" means bland meals, strict rules, and giving up the foods you love?
- Are you dreaming about the ultimate American road trip... but don't know where to start?
- Ever feel like kids ask questions you can't answer... and teens stop asking questions altogether?
- Have you ever felt like something is missing from the story you've been told?
- Still wasting hours on Excel tasks that should take minutes?

## Follow-Up Lines

- You're not alone.
- You're not the only one.
- And you're not doing anything wrong.
- Keep reading...

## Subheadline Patterns

- What if the right [book/guide/system] could [spark curiosity / create confidence / remove guesswork] — without [negative feeling]?
- What if you could finally stop [bad behavior], [bad workaround], and [stuck feeling]?
- What if you could [desired outcome] without [main objection]?
- What if [care/improvement/result] could actually be enjoyable, simple, and deeply satisfying?
